Amerika yijunditse u Bushinwa, umuyoboro ujyana gaz mu Burayi wahagaritswe: Ibigezweho mu ntambara ya Ukraine

Yanditswe na IGIHE
Kuya 11 July 2022 saa 12:30
Yasuwe :
Yavuzweho:

Umwe mu miyoboro ijyana gaz mu Burayi ivuye mu Burusiya wahagaritswe by’agateganyo, mu gihe hari gukorwa imirimo yo kuwusana nk’uko u Burusiya bwabitangaje.

Uyu muyoboro uzwi nka Nord Stream 1 ujyana gaz mu bihugu birimo u Budage, unyuze mu nyanja ya Baltique.

Ikigo Nord Stream AG gishinzwe imirimo y’uwo muyoboro cyatangaje ko kuwusana bizamara iminsi igera ku icumi, uhereye kuri uyu wa Mbere. Bivuze ko iyo minsi izashira nta gaz iwunyuramo.

Icyo kigo cyatangaje ko mbere yo gufunga uwo muyoboro cyabanje kuganira n’abafatanyabikorwa bacyo, nk’uko RT yabitangaje.

Mu kwezi gushize, u Burusiya bwagabanyije gaz yanyuzwaga muri uwo muyoboro ku kigero cya 60% bitewe n’uko imashini itanga ingufu zisunika gaz (turbine) yagiye gusanirwa muri Canada, mu gihe abandi bavuga ko ari impamvu za Politiki.

Canada yamaze gutangaza ko iyo mashini yasanwe kandi igiye gusubizwa u Burusiya, nubwo Ukraine yo yabyamaganye, isaba ko iyo mashini iguma muri Canada.

Mu myaka ishize, iyo Nord Stream 1 yagiraga ikibazo hongerwaga gaz inyura mu yindi miyoboro inyura muri Ukraine na Pologne.

Ukraine yasabye abaturage kuva bwangu mu duce twigaruriwe n’u Burusiya

Leta ya Ukraine yasabye abaturage bayo kuva mu bice bya Donbas mu burasirazuba bw’igihugu, ahaherutse kwigarurirwa n’u Burusiya mu ntambara imaze hafi amezi atanu.

Minisitiri w’Intebe wungirije, Irina Vereshchuk, yatangaje ko bari gutegura igitero simusiga kizirukana ingabo z’u Burusiya muri ako gace.

Yagize ati "Sinzi ngo bizaba ari ibitero bingana iki, ariko abagore n’abana ntibagomba kuba bahari. Ni ibigaragara ko hazabaho kurasana gukomeye, turagira inama abaturage bacu kuhava."

Minisitiri w’Ingabo Alexey Reznikov yatangaje ko bahawe itegeko ryo kwitegura ibitero bikomeye, ndetse ko hari ingabo zisaga miliyoni ziryamiye amajanja ngo zambarire urugamba isaha n’isaha.

U Burusiya bumaze iminsi bufasha uduce twa Luhansk na Donetsk turi muri Donbas no kutwemera nka Leta zigenga, ndetse buvuga ko abaturage batwo bafitanye amateka yihariye n’Abarusiya, ari nacyo bashinja Ukraine kubangira.

Leta ya Ukraine yasabye abaturage bayo kuva mu bice bya Donbas

Ukraine yashimiye inkunga irimo iya gisirikare yahawe na Amerika

Minisitiri w’Ububanyi n’Amahanga wa Ukraine, Dmytro Kuleba yashimiye Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika ku nkunga irimo iy’ibikoresho bya gisirikare yabahaye mu mpera z’icyumweru gishize.

Inkunga Amerika yatanze irimo ijyanye n’amafaranga, ibyo gufasha abaturage bakuwe mu byabo n’intambara n’iya gisirikare, mu gihe imirwano ikomeje hagati y’u Burusiya na Ukraine.

Ku wa Gatanu Antony Blinken ushinzwe ububanyi n’amahanga bwa Amerika yavuze ko bagiye guha Ukraine miliyoni 368 z’Amadolari, zo gufasha abakuwe mu byabo n’intambara.

Ayo kandi yaje akurikira inkunga ya miliyoni 400 z’Amadolari ingabo za Amerika zahaye Ukraine, harimo imbunda zirasa kure cyane za HIMARS, intwaro ziremreye n’ibindi.

Amerika mu byumweru bitatu bishize yiyemeje guha Ukraine inkunga mu by’umutekano, igera kuri miliyari 2.2 z’amadolari.

U Burusiya bwigambye guhanura indege eshatu za Ukraine

U Burusiya bwigambye guhanura kajugujugu za gisirikare zagurukaga hejuru y’ikirere cya Donetsk, agace kari mu two u Burusiya bwigaruriye mu burasirazuba bwa Ukraine.

Umuvugizi wa Minisiteri y’Ingabo y’u Burusiya, Lt Gen Igor Konashenkov, yavuze ko ingabo z’igihugu cye zashwanyaguje ububiko bubiri bwari burimo ibifaru byakorewe muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, byari bibitse mu gace ka Kostiantynivka, mu majyaruguru ya Donetsk.

Ni mu gihe ingabo za Ukraine zo zatangaje ko umusirikare umwe ari we waguye muri ako gace kuri iki Cyumweru.

Konashenkov kandi yavuze ko ingabo z’u Burusiya zarashe ku bubiko bw’intwaro mu mujyi wa Sloviansk, bagahitana abasaga ijana bahakoraga.

Amerika yijunditse u Bushinwa bugicuditse n’u Burusiya

Antony Blinken ushinzwe ububanyi n’amahanga bwa Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika yihanangirije u Bushinwa bugicuditse n’u Burusiya, nyuma y’uko ibihugu byinshi by’i Burayi bibuvanyeho amaboko kubera intambara bumazemo iminsi muri Ukraine.

Blinken yabitangaje ku wa Gatandatu, nyuma y’amasaha atanu aganira na mugenzi we w’u Bushinwa, Wang Yi.

Mu kiganiro yagiranye n’abanyamakuru aharimo kubera inama y’ibihugu 20 bikize (G20), muri Indonesia, yavuze ko ibihugu byinshi muri G20 bishyigikiye Ukraine, nyamara u Bushinwa bwo bwakomeje gutsimbarara.

Ati "Beijing ikomeza kuvuga ko ntaho ibogamiye, ni ibintu bisa nk’ibidashoboka kutagira uruhande ubogamiraho mu bushotoranyi nk’ubu."

Yavuze ko bitumvikana uburyo u Bushinwa buvuga ko ntaho bubogamiye, mu gihe abaturage ba Ukraine basaga miliyoni 11 bamaze guhunga.
